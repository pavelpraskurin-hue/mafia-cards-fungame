HOUSE RULES

How to play

  1. Deal. You receive one random secret card. Each bot privately receives a different card.
  2. Night. If your role has an action, choose a target. Bots make their own hidden decisions.
  3. Day. Type anything in chat. The logical, paranoid, and aggressive bots respond, then everyone votes.
  4. Win. Town wins by eliminating all Mafia. Mafia wins when they equal or outnumber everyone else.

Choose one player as narrator, or let an eliminated player narrate after the first day.
